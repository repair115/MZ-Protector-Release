# M.Z Protector — Secure No-Log VPN for Android

**Bypass censorship. Protect your privacy. Browse freely.**

M.Z Protector is a professional anti-censorship and anti-filtering VPN application for Android. It helps you bypass ISP restrictions, overcome operator-level blocks, and access the open internet securely — without storing any logs of your activity.

## Features

- No-Log Policy — Your privacy is paramount. We keep zero logs of your browsing activity.
- Advanced Anti-Censorship — Powerful engine that bypasses sophisticated filtering systems and ISP restrictions.
- High Performance — Optimized for speed and reliability on all network types (4G, 5G, Wi-Fi).
- DNS-over-HTTPS — Encrypted DNS queries prevent eavesdropping on your browsing destinations.
- Split Tunneling — Choose which apps go through the VPN and which connect directly.
- Android Native — Built specifically for Android with a clean, modern interface.
- Privacy-First Design — No tracking, no analytics, no data collection.
- Anti-Filtering — Defeats advanced DPI (Deep Packet Inspection) and operator-level blocks.

## Why Choose M.Z Protector?

Most VPN apps log your data or use unsafe protocols. M.Z Protector is built from the ground up with privacy as the core principle:

- Zero activity logs
- Zero connection logs
- Open-source transparency
- Regular security updates
- Persian-language interface support
